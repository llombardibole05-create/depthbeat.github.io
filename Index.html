What Is DepthBeat?

DepthBeat is a groundbreaking biotech initiative focused on human survivability in the most hostile environments on Earth — and eventually, beyond it. Founded by Malachi, a 20-year-old heart transplant candidate, DepthBeat began as a personal mission to survive, and has since evolved into a platform for life-preserving AI and biomedical systems.

Our Flagship Technologies

Revenant Mk I

An ICU-grade, AI-powered exosuit capable of monitoring, predicting, and responding to cardiac crises in real time. Fully autonomous underwater version designed for high-risk environments.

DepthBeat Kids

The world’s smallest internal cardiac monitoring system for children and transplant patients. Real-time vitals. Bio-integrated. Biodegradable on command.

Revenant Apex

The dry ops CEO-class armor. Silent. Sleek. Designed for protection, posture, AI communication, and leadership — without bulk or aggression.

Driven by NOX
